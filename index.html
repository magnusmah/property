About This Unit

This is a 1,668 sq ft dual key corner unit at Tropez Residences, Danga Bay, Johor Bahru, available for rent at RM 3,000 per month. The unit is located on the 24th floor of Block B and offers a permanent unobstructed view facing the Istana Bukit Serene royal palace grounds to the North East.

At 1,668 sq ft, this is one of the largest units available at Tropez Residences — significantly larger than the standard units in the development which range from 900 to 1,100 sq ft. It is a freehold property developed by Tropicana Corporation and completed in 2014.

Comparable 1,668 sqft units at Tropez are listed at RM 3,500/mo. This unit is offered at RM 3,000/mo — RM 500 below market for the same size.

Why Dual Key?

A dual key unit contains two fully independent living spaces under a single strata title. Each space has its own entrance on the same corridor. The main unit and the studio are completely self-contained. You can occupy the main unit and rent out the studio for an estimated RM 900 to RM 1,000 per month, reducing your effective net rent to as low as RM 2,000 per month. This layout suits investor-occupiers, extended families who need privacy, and Singapore-based professionals looking for a base in Johor Bahru.

Location

Tropez Residences is located at Danga Bay Jalan Danga 2, Danga Bay, Johor Bahru, Johor, Malaysia (postal code 80200). The development sits along the Straits of Johor waterfront in the Danga Bay precinct. It is developed by Tropicana Corporation and comprises three towers — Tower A and Tower B each with 38 storeys, and Tower C with 29 storeys. Total of 1,149 units.
